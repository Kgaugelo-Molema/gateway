Background information

Gateway Accounting Systems is a software company founded in 2004 by Kgaugelo Molema that deals with automated business solutions for all types of business. The company was inspired by two software projects that started to be developed over seven years ago, which were mainly built to meet client's requirements. Both tools have over 39 production releases and still undergoing enhancements to keep track with the fast growing information technology environment.

Company policy

Terms and Conditions

Gateway Accounting Systems has strict policies that go with the development of automated business solutions for all clients. All organisations' main goal is to maximise profits, and to achieve such a goal efficient business processes need to be employed to increase the production of its goods and services, giving more opportunities to job seeking individuals. To develop such solutions that will always meet client's requirements the development team has to be equipped with the necessary skill, knowledge and experience which the organisation always make a prerequisite before any development resource is employed.

Software development

Gateway Accounting Systems uses enterprise level methods to when a project is being implemented in order to suite the client's present and future needs. The solution will not only be built to meet the current client requirements but also have room for future enhancements, therefore it is important that during Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) the following must be prerequisites must be met for every resource employed.

  • Resources must work together towards one objective which is to give the client good quality solutions.
  • Segregation of duties amongst resources must be put in place and grouped in a form of teams.
  • A project plan must be drawn to reflect the estimated time span of the SDLC.
  • There must be an analysis team that analyses the needs of the client and compile documents to specify all the business processes that will meet all the client's requirements (Systems analysis phase).
  • The project plan must be revisited to amend any time frames specified by the project plan since the analysis documents will give a clear view of what needs to done.
  • Tasks will be delegated amongst the development team based on their knowledge and experiences as software developers to ensure Total Quality Management (TQM).
  • The implementation phase will commence for a period specified in the project plan in order to keep the project under its budget and not exceed it.
  • Once the implementation is done a team of software testers must be in place to test what has been implemented and obtain satisfaction that the software solution meets all the requirements of the client.
  • The software project will be concluded at the time of deployment to the client's production environment, to be used by trained staff and carry the day-to-day operations of the business.
